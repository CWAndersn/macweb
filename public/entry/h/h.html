WELCOME TO HEX

Welcome to MacGregor’s H Entry - also known as Hex! This website’s still under construction but feel free to poke around. We’re a group of hackers, builders, and thinkers that comprise the largest entry in MacGregor. What are things that the H Entry Fire Hazards do together?

  • Hanging out with G entry
  • Cooking all sorts of weird smelling foods, especially at 2 AM
  • Heading to MacCon, conveniently located right below us
    • RIP MacCon, who cares tbh - 2017
  • Being Fire Hazards... oops
  • Playing Mario Party 4, Zelda, Super Smash Bros, and other classics
  • Watching Football/the Oscars/various movies on MacG’s largest TV
  • Not sleeping, “sleeping”, or actually sleeping
  • PING PONG TOURNAMENTS (in the MacGregor Game Room)
  • Talking about life and the state of the world
  • Enjoying some alone time because that’s also perfectly ok
